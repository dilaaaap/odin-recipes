Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Everyone loves eggs.

If you don't love eggs then you get to learn how to make them anyways because who doesn't like eggs?

The answer is crackheads. Crackheads hate eggs.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Pan
  • Bowl and fork

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Crack eggs in bowl
  2. Whisk Eggs
  3. Pour eggs in hot pan over stove.
  4. Stir eggs in pan like a brainlet until they are solid.
